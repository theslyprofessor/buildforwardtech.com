# Thematic Technology Investment Vehicle — Research Notes

## 1. Realistic Fund Structure Options

### A. Traditional Mutual Fund (Open-End Fund)

- **Regulatory framework:** Must register under the Investment Company Act of 1940 and the Securities Act of 1933 with the SEC.
- **Minimum capital:** No legal minimum, but practically requires **$20–50M AUM** to be economically viable. Fund administration, legal, compliance, audit, and custody costs run **$150K–$500K/year** as a baseline.
- **Timeline to launch:** 6–12 months for SEC registration (Form N-1A filing), plus legal setup.
- **Verdict for a two-person team:** **Not practical as a starting point.**

### B. Exchange-Traded Fund (ETF)

- **White-label / turnkey ETF platforms:** This is the realistic path:
  - **Tidal Financial Group**, **Exchange Traded Concepts**, **Alpha Architect**, **Empowered Funds**
  - They handle SEC registration, compliance, trading, custody, AP relationships
  - You provide the index methodology or active strategy
  - **Cost:** ~$150K–$300K/year in platform fees + basis points on AUM
- **Minimum capital:** Seed capital of **$2.5–5M** to launch, needs **$25–50M AUM** to be sustainable.
- **Timeline:** 3–6 months via a white-label platform.
- **Verdict:** Plausible medium-term goal (12–24 months) after building track record.

### C. Hedge Fund (Private Fund)

- **Regulatory framework:** Exempt from 1940 Act via 3(c)(1) or 3(c)(7) exemptions. Comply with Reg D (Rule 506(b) or 506(c)).
- **Minimum capital:** **No regulatory minimum.** Can launch with **$500K–$2M** from friends, family, early believers.
- **Key costs:**
  - Legal setup: **$15K–$50K** (PPM, operating agreement, subscription docs)
  - Fund administrator: **$1K–$3K/month**
  - Audit: **$10K–$25K/year**
- **Investor limits:** 3(c)(1) allows up to 99 accredited investors
- **Fee structure:** Classic 2/20, emerging managers often 1/10 or 1.5/15
- **Verdict:** **Most realistic fund structure for our team.**

### D. Registered Investment Adviser (RIA) + Separately Managed Accounts (SMAs)

- **Minimum capital:** **No minimum.** Start managing money immediately after registration.
- **Key costs:** $10K–$30K Year 1 (legal, compliance, software)
- **Custodian options:** Schwab, Fidelity, **Altruist** (built for small RIAs, no AUM minimum)
- **Revenue:** 0.50–1.50% of AUM
- **Verdict:** **Lowest barrier to entry. Ideal starting point.**

## 6. Key Regulatory Notes

- **Investment Advisers Act of 1940:** Governs RIAs. Form ADV. Fiduciary duty.
- **Regulation D (506(b)/506(c)):** Governs private fund offerings.
- **Form D:** File within 15 days of first sale.
- **State blue sky laws:** Notice filings required.
- **Below $100M AUM → register with state; $150M+ → SEC**
